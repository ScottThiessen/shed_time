Shed or shedding verb (short for woodshed) -- To practice ones instrument.

Shed Time is an online practice log to help musicians optimize their practice time. Once registered, aspiring musicians can browse our library of exercises and get to shedding! The instrument pages have guided routines with a checkbox next to each one. Simply practice the routines then check off the box! Our Shed Log page displays how long you have worked on specific exercises, the date you last worked on it and how many times you have completed it. This allows you to pinpoint precisely where to focus your energy so you can achieve your goals in the most efficient way possible.
